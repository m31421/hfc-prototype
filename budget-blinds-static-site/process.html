Our Process

  • Getting started is simple. You'll schedule a complimentary in-person or virtual consultation at a time that fits best in your schedule. Unlike buying online, you'll have a real design expert focused on your space, not a one-size-fits-all solution.

  • Our design consultant comes to you, taking time to understand your style and needs. We'll walk through the window treatments best suited for your home and present tailored options that fit your preferences.

  • Together, we'll finalize your selections so you feel confident in your decision. Then we'll take precise measurements and provide a detailed, upfront quote for your custom window treatments.

  • Your beautiful new upgrades happen with no stress. Sit back while our professionals handle expert installation. We'll walk you through operation and care, and your local team is always a call away if you need support later.
